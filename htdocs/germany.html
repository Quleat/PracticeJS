  Germany, where lederhosen meet laptops and fairy-tale castles share the skyline with cutting-edge skyscrapers! Picture yourself savoring a pretzel the size of your head in Bavaria, or getting lost (in a good way) in the winding streets of Berlin, a city that's cooler than your cousin who spent a summer in Ibiza. Wander through the Black Forest, where the trees are tall, the cake is layered, and the cuckoo clocks are always right on time. Sip a stein of beer at Oktoberfest, try to keep up with the locals' toasts, and dance like no one’s watching (because they’re all dancing too!)


  From the scenic Rhine Valley to the trendy cafes of Hamburg, Germany is your ticket to a trip filled with unforgettable moments and probably more sausages than you ever thought possible. Willkommen to the land of beer, bratwurst, and a whole lot of fun!
